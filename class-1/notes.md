## Arithmatic Operator :

- (+): Addition ex: 2+3 = 5
- (-): Substraction ex: 5-2 = 3
- (*): Multiplication ex: 5*2 = 10
- (/): Division ex: 10/2 = 5
- (%): Modules ex: 7%3 = 1
- (\* \*): Exponent ex: 2\*\*3 = 8
- (++): Increment ex: ++1 = 2
- (--): Decrement ex: --1 =0

## Assignment Operators:

- Assignment (=)
- Addition Assignment (+=)
- Substraction Assignment (-=)
- Multiplication Assignment (\*=)
- Division Assignment (/=)
- Remainder Assignment (%=)

## Comparison Operators:

- Equal to (==)
- Not equal to (!=)
- Strict equal to (===)
- Strict not equal to (!==)
- Greater than (>)
- Less than (<)
- Greater than or equal to (>=)
- Less than or equal to (<=)

## Logical Operators:

- Logical AND (&&)
- Logical OR (||)
- Logical NOT (!)

## Ternary Operator :

- Conditional Operator (?:)
